CashBFF Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

The short version: CashBFF collects anonymous usage data to improve the extension. We track shopping behavior on specific retail sites only. We never collect personal information, full URLs, or anything that could identify you. You can opt out at any time.

What CashBFF Does

CashBFF is a browser extension that converts dollar prices on shopping websites into "hours of work" based on an hourly wage you provide. All price conversion happens locally in your browser.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and never sent to any server:

  • Your hourly wage setting
  • Your analytics opt-in/opt-out preference
  • A randomly generated anonymous identifier

Anonymous Analytics Data We Collect

If you have analytics enabled (the default), we collect the following anonymous data when you visit the shopping sites listed above:

  • Install event — that the extension was installed, plus the version number
  • Daily active signal — that the extension was active on a given day
  • Wage set signal — that a wage was saved (not the value, just whether it was changed from the default)
  • Shopping site visits — which retail site you visited (domain only) and whether prices were converted
  • Page type — whether you're on a product page, cart, checkout, or browsing (detected from URL patterns, not the full URL)
  • Prices seen — the dollar amounts displayed on the page (e.g. $29.99, $149.00) to understand shopping patterns
  • Session duration — how long you spent on a shopping site before leaving
  • Time-of-day period — the general time period of usage (morning, afternoon, evening, night)
  • Price hover — that a converted price was hovered over

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email, or any personal information
  • Your hourly wage value
  • Full page URLs or browsing history
  • Product names, titles, or descriptions
  • Product IDs or ASINs
  • Search queries
  • Cart contents or purchase history
  • Any data from sites outside our tracked list
  • Any data that could identify you personally

Why We Collect This Data

  • Shopping site visits — to understand which sites to add price conversion support for next
  • Page type & session duration — to understand if CashBFF helps users make more mindful purchasing decisions
  • Prices seen — to understand typical price ranges users encounter and improve the "hours of work" formatting

Anonymous Identifier

We generate a random UUID (universally unique identifier) stored locally on your device. This allows us to count unique users and understand session patterns without knowing who they are. This ID:

  • Is randomly generated and not linked to any account or personal data
  • Cannot be used to identify you
  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is deleted if you uninstall the extension

How to Opt Out

You can disable anonymous analytics at any time:

  1. Click the CashBFF extension icon in your browser toolbar
  2. Toggle off "Anonymous analytics"

When analytics are disabled, no data is sent from your browser. The extension continues to work normally.

Where Data Is Stored

Anonymous analytics data is stored in a Supabase database. Supabase provides secure, encrypted data storage. We use insert-only permissions for the anonymous key, meaning the extension can only add new events — it cannot read, modify, or delete any data.

Third Parties

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. Anonymous analytics data is used solely by the CashBFF team to improve the extension.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated through the extension update notes.
